About FPTV Skills Challenge 2026

Showcasing TVET excellence and preparing students for the workforce of tomorrow.

Empowering TVET, Shaping the Future

The FPTV Skills Challenge (FPTVSC) is proudly organized by the Faculty of Technical and Vocational Education (FPTV), Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia, in conjunction with Hari TVET Negara. This annual event reflects FPTV’s commitment to producing graduates who are job-ready, adaptable, and innovative.

Through hands-on competitions judged by industry experts, students demonstrate technical knowledge, creativity, and collaboration that directly mirror workplace demands. The challenge provides real-world exposure, strengthens industry–academia ties, and promotes TVET as a premier pathway to professional success.

  • ✅ Strengthening industry collaboration
  • ✅ Enhancing practical, job-ready skills
  • ✅ Promoting innovation and creativity in TVET

Growth & Impact

Started in 2024

Launched in FPTV, UTHM alongside Hari TVET Negara, marking the foundation of FPTVSC.

Rising Participation

Pre-qualification rounds introduced with only selected participants reaching the final stage.

Expanding Reach

Currently FPTV-focused, but expanding into other faculties within UTHM and MTUN.

Industry Relevance

Endorsed by experts, ensuring competitions mirror real workplace skills and industry demands.

Our Journey

2024 – The Beginning

Launched at FPTV, UTHM with Hari TVET Negara, marking the start of a platform for future-ready graduates.

2026 – Towards Expansion

Expanding to include other faculties within UTHM, setting the stage for national-level participation.

MTUN Growth

Planned expansion across MTUN (UTHM, UTeM, UniMAP, UMP), building a national platform for TVET excellence.

Future – ASEAN & Beyond

Aiming to grow into a regional platform, showcasing Malaysian TVET talent across ASEAN and globally.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To foster technical excellence, innovation, and industry-relevant skills among students.

Vision: To become the leading platform for showcasing and nurturing TVET talent in Malaysia and beyond.
