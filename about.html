About FPTV Skills Challenge 2026

Showcasing TVET excellence and preparing students for the workforce of tomorrow.

Empowering TVET, Shaping the Future

The FPTV Skills Challenge (FPTVSC), started in 2024 at FPTV, UTHM, has grown rapidly with increasing sponsor support. While it began with FPTV students, plans are underway to expand participation to other faculties and eventually MTUN institutions.

Aligned with Hari TVET Negara, the event reflects Malaysia’s commitment to advancing technical and vocational education, fostering innovation, and preparing students for Industry 4.0 and beyond.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To strengthen industry collaboration, enhance job-ready skills, and promote creativity and innovation among TVET students.

Vision

To position TVET as a premier pathway to professional success and empower students for leadership in Industry 4.0.

Our Journey

2024 – The Beginning

Launched at FPTV, UTHM with student-only participation. Sponsors began to recognize TVET’s potential.

2025 – Growing Recognition

Sponsorship grew as industries saw the value. Participants went through pre-qualification before the finals.

2026 – Expanding Horizons

Plans to include other faculties within UTHM. Discussions underway to open participation to MTUN universities.

Why FPTVSC Matters

Innovation in Action

Students bring fresh solutions to technical challenges, showing the power of creativity in TVET fields.

Collaboration & Teamwork

Competitions encourage teamwork, communication, and leadership — essential workplace skills.

Excellence Recognized

Performance is evaluated by professionals, giving students recognition and exposure to industry standards.

Towards Expansion

FPTVSC will grow beyond FPTV, with participation expanding across UTHM faculties and eventually to the Malaysian Technical University Network (MTUN), comprising UTHM, UTeM, UniMAP, and UMP. This step strengthens the platform for TVET excellence nationwide.

In the future, FPTVSC aims to attract ASEAN participants, becoming a hub for international TVET collaboration and innovation.
