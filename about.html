About the Challenge

FPTV Skills Challenge 2026 celebrates innovation, skills, and the spirit of TVET excellence. Hosted by Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM), this prestigious event brings together talented youths nationwide to compete in hands-on skill-based challenges. Over 70 participants across 7 diverse trades will showcase their technical mastery, creativity, and teamwork during 3 action-packed days. More than a competition, it is a platform for inspiring future industry leaders and advancing Malaysia’s TVET agenda in the era of Industry 4.0.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To foster technical and vocational excellence through real-world competitions that enhance creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving among future industry leaders.

Vision: To be a leading national TVET competition that empowers youth, nurtures industry-relevant skills, and supports Malaysia’s aspirations in the era of IR 4.0.

Objectives

  • Provide a competitive platform for showcasing technical and vocational skills.
  • Bridge the gap between education and industry through hands-on challenges.
  • Encourage innovation, problem-solving, and entrepreneurial thinking among participants.
  • Foster collaboration across institutions, industries, and communities.
  • Cultivate a culture of excellence and resilience aligned with national TVET goals and SDG4.

Impact

FPTV Skills Challenge strengthens Malaysia’s TVET ecosystem by preparing skilled graduates for the workforce, promoting lifelong learning, and elevating global competitiveness. The 2026 edition will bring together 70 participants across 7 skill trades in a dynamic 3-day showcase of excellence hosted at UTHM.
