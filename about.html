About FPTV Skills Challenge 2026

Showcasing TVET excellence and preparing students for the workforce of tomorrow.

Background

The FPTV Skills Challenge (FPTVSC) began in 2024, hosted by the Faculty of Technical and Vocational Education (FPTV), Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM). Since its inception, the event has seen growing support from sponsors who recognize the potential of TVET-driven talent development.

Initially designed for FPTV students, the competition is poised to expand participation to MTUN institutions and beyond, strengthening its role as a national platform for technical excellence.
