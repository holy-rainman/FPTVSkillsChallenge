About FPTV Skills Challenge 2026

The Faculty of Technical and Vocational Education (FPTV), Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia, proudly organizes the FPTV Skills Challenge as part of Hari TVET Negara. This initiative showcases the commitment of the faculty to empower TVET education and prepare students with real-world, job-ready skills.
