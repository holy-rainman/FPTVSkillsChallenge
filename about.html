About the Challenge

Celebrating innovation, skills, and the spirit of TVET excellence — FPTV Skills Challenge 2026 unites young talents from across Malaysia.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To foster technical and vocational excellence through real-world competitions that enhance creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving among future industry leaders.

Vision: To be a leading national TVET competition that empowers youth, nurtures industry-relevant skills, and supports Malaysia’s aspirations in the era of IR 4.0.

Objectives

  • To provide a competitive platform for showcasing technical and vocational skills.
  • To bridge the gap between education and industry through hands-on challenges.
  • To encourage innovation, problem-solving, and entrepreneurial thinking among participants.
  • To foster collaboration across institutions, industries, and communities.
  • To cultivate a culture of excellence and resilience aligned with national TVET goals and SDG4.

Key Stakeholders

The challenge is organized by Faculty of Technical and Vocational Education (FPTV), UTHM, with support from industry partners, educational institutions, and national bodies including BPLTV, WorldSkills Malaysia, and leading TVET-driven industries.

Impact

FPTV Skills Challenge has a transformative impact by preparing skilled graduates for the workforce, promoting lifelong learning, and enhancing Malaysia’s competitiveness globally. The 2026 edition will gather over 70 participants across 7 skill categories, competing in a dynamic 3-day event hosted at UTHM.
