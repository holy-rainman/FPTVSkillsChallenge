About FPTV Skills Challenge 2026

Showcasing TVET excellence and preparing students for the workforce of tomorrow.

Empowering TVET, Shaping the Future

The FPTV Skills Challenge (FPTVSC) is proudly organized by the Faculty of Technical and Vocational Education (FPTV), Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia, in conjunction with Hari TVET Negara.

Through hands-on competitions judged by industry experts, students demonstrate technical knowledge, creativity, and collaboration that directly mirror workplace demands.

Why FPTVSC Matters

Innovation in Action

Students bring fresh solutions to technical challenges, showing the power of creativity in TVET fields.

Collaboration & Teamwork

Competitions encourage teamwork, communication, and leadership — essential workplace skills.

Excellence Recognized

Performance is evaluated by professionals, giving students recognition and exposure to industry standards.
